The Packaging Penalty
The Problem

Every new bottle costs you up to 30% more than it should.

Every time you buy a bottle of detergent, up to 30% of what you pay covers the cost of the plastic bottle — not the liquid inside it. You use the product once, throw away the bottle, and pay the packaging premium again next time.

This is the Packaging Penalty — and it's costing Egyptian households billions of pounds every year. Natex built a smarter system.
