Who We Are

At the intersection of consumer goods, retail innovation, and smart technology.

Natex is an Egyptian FMCG manufacturer specializing in home care, personal care, and household essentials. We are the makers of BBL — Beyond Basic Labs — Egypt's leading refillable cleaning and hygiene brand.

Founded within the NATEX Group, our manufacturing roots give us tight control over quality and cost; our retail network and proprietary vending technology give us a direct line to the consumer.

Our Story

How BBL began.

BBL started from a question: why does a bottle of detergent cost so much more than the liquid inside it?

01
The Question
Why does the bottle cost more than the product?

The Packaging Penalty: the 15–30% price premium consumers pay on every single-use bottle. Natex set out to break that cycle.

02
The Formula
Developed proprietary product formulas.

Manufactured to pharmaceutical-grade standards, optimized for Egyptian water conditions, Egyptian homes, and Egyptian consumers.

03
The Technology
Built smart Detergent Vending Machines.

IoT-enabled refill stations with real-time remote monitoring, ±1% dispensing accuracy, and full touchscreen interfaces. No app required. No membership needed.

04
The Network
Opened refill retail branches across Cairo.

New Cairo, 1st Settlement, Al-Zaytoun, New Nozha. Multiple retail stations, a growing online store, and a franchise model designed to scale nationally.

05
2026 — Now
National franchise expansion underway.

45+ SKUs spanning 6 product lines, 40,000+ social media followers, and a national franchise model in active rollout. BBL is scaling fast.
